An introduction from the author

Amie Lockwood

During my time as a recruitment specialist and working with various Government agencies on trying to crack the unemployment problem, I have noticed a few themes developing. I have also picked up a lot of tips and tricks to help people stand out and achieve the career they desperately want.

It is quite clear that schools are simply not equipping school leavers with the knowledge needed to get a job. The unemployed are also not being given the opportunity to learn modern job hunting skills. The job market is changing rapidly and we are on the verge of an unemployment epidemic.

How To Get A Job is the end result of years in the job hunting industry, it gives practical tips to help anybody get the job that they want. The days of mailing out CV's are gone, the modern job hunter has to be smart, this book will set you on your way.

Available June 2017
